How to manage podcasts

Create your own podcast library, and listen through MC or upload to your handheld player. Podcast Feeds are added under Podcasts in the Tree on the left. Right-click each Feed to set download requirements. Click a subscribed Feed to see a list of Podcast Episodes in the Content Pane. These can be individually clicked for immediate streaming or they can be downloaded to your PC by pressing Download in the Episode Status column. Downloaded Episodes are shown as On Disk.
