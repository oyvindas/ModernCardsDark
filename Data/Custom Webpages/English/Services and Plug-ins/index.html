Plug-ins & Services

What are Plug-ins?

Small software components that add functionality, and extend the capabilities of larger software programs. MC plug-ins provide additional features and allow compatibility with other software programs and hardware devices. MC comes loaded with certain plug-ins, but others can be downloaded from the MC Plug-ins page.

Services Features

The Services feature allows you to open popular online services or stores within MC, such as music, audiobook or video stores, and lets you browse and buy for your media collection.

Managing Services

Go to Tools> Options > Services, and select your services. You can then opt to show or hide these services at any time.

Server Plug-ins included with MC

Media Network

Provides on-demand streaming of your files (music and images) from a Server to a Client. The Server is the PC that makes its library available to another computer, while the Client is the computer that sees those files. It allows you to share the library from one computer to another computer, or across the Internet. Setup Info

TiVo Server

Lets those of you who have the TiVo® Series2™ or newer play music and photos from an MC library on your PC's through TiVo's Home Media feature. It allows you to browse and play music playlists and photo slideshows that have already been created using MC's unbeatable organization features. Setup Info

DLNA Server

Will connect to a variety of DLNA devices, which let you stream audio, video and images to your home theater or TV. Setup Info
